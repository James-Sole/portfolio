About me...

I am James Sole, a software developer passionate about bringing ideas to life. During my college education, I worked on mechanical projects and felt limited in the aspect of coding so I decided to go to school to learn how to program. I have worked in retail and currently still hold a position as a brand ambassador increasing the sales of our product by an average of 40%. Now that I have programming skills, I want to further innovate the world of technology by solving tough problems with practical solutions.

Have a project that can help people or a company built to help people? Contact me at James.sole20@gmail.com.
